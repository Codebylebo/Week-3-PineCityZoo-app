WILD THINGS COFEE SHOP

Fuel your adventure with a wild selection of freshly brewed coffee, exotic teas, and jungle-inspired pastries! Whether you need a morning boost or a cozy spot to unwind, Wild Things Coffee Shop is the perfect retreat.

Specialty Drinks:

  • Lion's Latte - A bold espresso blend with caramel notes, perfect for the fearless explorer.
  • Cheetah Chai - A spiced tea that's smooth, fast, and full of flavor.
  • Tropical Parrot Punch - A refreshing iced fruit infusion with mango, pineapple & citrus!
